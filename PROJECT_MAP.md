## Repository Overview

This repository presents a conceptual framework for reframing AI, AGI, and ASI risk.

**Central thesis:** The risk of superintelligent AI may lie not only in the level of intelligence, but in the combination of intelligence and the value system it optimizes. AI may function as a mirror and amplifier of the civilization that creates it.

**Proposed direction:** AI, AGI, and ASI should be evaluated through a framework that goes beyond narrow human-preference alignment to include Natural Law compatibility, long-term sustainability, and Artificial Wisdom.

**Important caveat:** This is a theoretical proposal, not a verified solution.

## Conceptual Flow

```
AI / AGI / ASI risk is real and serious
      ↓
Standard framing: capability → danger
      ↓
Proposed extension: capability × value system → danger
      ↓
Value system may be inherited from human civilization
      ↓
Human civilization may encode short-term / domination / extraction values
      ↓
AI as mirror and amplifier of civilization's value system
      ↓
Proposed response: evaluate objectives, not only capability
      ↓
Artificial Wisdom: evaluate whether goals should be pursued
      ↓
Six Principles: Natural Law / Harmony / Circulation / Structure / Order / Wa
      ↓
Conceptual framework pending extensive research and validation
      ↓
Human oversight and democratic governance remain non-negotiable
```
